    • The lack of jobs in Angola

    Angola faces significant challenges related to job availability. Between the end of 2022 and the end of 2023, over 550,000 new workers joined the labor force, but only 10,000 jobs were added. Urban and youth unemployment rates surged to 42% and 58%, respectively, by the end of 2023, up from 39% and 53% the previous year1. This situation highlights the pressing need for policies and initiatives to promote job creation and address unemployment in the country.
